## Book These Ahead

!!! reserve "BasBas Kulma — Helsinki"
    Hardest table in Helsinki. Max 30 days out. Bar counter sometimes has walk-in seats.
    
    [:material-web: basbas.fi/kulma](https://basbas.fi/kulma/en/){ .md-button } · Call +358 44 230 5900

!!! reserve "LKK (Luovuus kukkii kaaoksesta) — Helsinki"
    Tiny creative restaurant. Books fast. Open Thu–Sun + Mon from 5 PM.
    
    [:material-web: Reserve on Quandoo](https://www.quandoo.fi/en/place/luovuus-kukkii-kaaoksesta-90397){ .md-button } · Email info@luovuuskukkiikaaoksesta.fi

!!! reserve "Löyly Sauna — Helsinki"
    €27/person for 2-hour session. Book early morning slot. Bring swimsuits. Restaurant is walk-in during summer.
    
    [:material-web: Book sauna session](https://varaus.asio.fi/onlinekalenteri/loyly/guest.php?ss_lang=eng){ .md-button }

!!! reserve "Bryggeloftet & Stuene — Bergen"
    Request upstairs (Bryggeloftet) for harbor views. Family-run since 1910. Get the fish soup.
    
    [:material-web: bryggeloftet.no](https://www.bryggeloftet.no){ .md-button } · Call +47 55 30 20 70

!!! reserve "Kødbyens Fiskebar — Copenhagen"
    Michelin Bib Gourmand. They turn people away without reservations. Meatpacking District.
    
    [:material-web: fiskebaren.dk](https://fiskebaren.dk/en/){ .md-button } · Call +45 32 15 56 56
